Drug Discovery & Research

Our world-class research team utilizes AI-driven molecular modeling, high-throughput screening, and computational chemistry to identify promising drug candidates faster than ever before.

GMP Manufacturing

Global Supply Chain

Clinical Trials Management

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What therapeutic areas does STACKLY PHARMA cover?

We cover cardiovascular, oncology, neurology, respiratory, immunology, and several other therapeutic areas with 500+ products in our portfolio.

Are your manufacturing facilities GMP certified?

Yes, all our production facilities are GMP-certified and comply with FDA, EMA, and WHO standards with regular audits and inspections.

How can I partner with STACKLY PHARMA?

Visit our Contact page to reach our business development team. We welcome partnerships with hospitals, pharmacies, distributors, and research institutions.

Do you offer contract manufacturing services?

Yes, we provide contract manufacturing (CMO) and contract development and manufacturing (CDMO) services for pharmaceutical companies globally.

What quality certifications do you hold?

We hold ISO 9001, ISO 14001, FDA GMP, EU GMP, WHO GMP certifications, and are regularly audited by international regulatory bodies.
